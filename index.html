TSUGI - A Framework for Building PHP-Based Learning Tools

Update: Tsugi has initial support for IMS LTI 2.0 Tool Registration, Outcomes, and Settings.

Welcome to the www.tsugi.org project. Its goal is to build a scalable multi-tenant "tool" hosting environment based on the emerging IMS standards.
